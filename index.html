What is Pact-Man?

Pact-Man is an AI-powered negotiation game. You play as a Startup Founder seeking Series A funding from an AI venture capitalist who negotiates back.

The investment amount is fixed at $100M — what you're negotiating is everything else. Both sides are trying to maximize their score across 5 deal terms, but your score sheets are different. You'll need to figure out where to push and where to concede.

Both parties have a BATNA (walk-away threshold) of 30 points — if your final score is below that, you're better off with no deal.

Your score sheet below shows how many points each option is worth to you. No need to memorize it — you'll have access to it in the sidebar during the negotiation.
